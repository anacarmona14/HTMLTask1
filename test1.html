What's free will?

Free will: is the ability to make choices and decisions that are not predetermined or predetermined by external forces.

It is a concept that has been debated for centuries, but is considered by many to be an integral part of human life. Free will allows individuals to choose their own paths and make decisions based on their personal preferences, values, and morals.

What does the bible say?

The Bible: Generally speaks positively about free will. The biblical writers believed that humans have the power to choose their own destiny and that the choice to live a life of obedience to God gives us the greatest joy and satisfaction. For example, in Deuteronomy 30:19-20, the Lord says: “I call heaven and earth as witness today against you, that I have set before you life and death, blessing and cursing; therefore choose life, that both you and your descendants may live.” This passage emphasizes our ability to make choices and provides encouragement to make good choices.

What does it say about our destiny?

Free will suggests that...: It suggests that our destiny is in our own hands, and that we have the power to choose our own paths. We can determine our own future by the choices we make, both big and small. Every decision we make affects how our life will unfold, and ultimately, we create our own destiny through the choices we make.

What does pop culture say about how we asume 'Free will'?

Pop culture often uses...: It often uses themes of free will to explore complex questions about morality, choice, and responsibility. In films, books, and television shows, characters are often faced with difficult decisions that have significant consequences.

Through these stories, pop culture attempts to portray how decisions can shape the future and help viewers understand the power of free will.

Final thoughts

    Let's take a final look
  • Free will is usally understood as the key to crafting our own future

Free will is usally understood as the key to crafting our own future

In Today's world it's assumed that free will gives us the power to make decisions that can shape our future. That we have the freedom to choose our own paths, from small choices like what to eat for dinner, to larger ones like where to go to college or who to marry.

Ultimately, free will is supposed to provide us with the ability to determine our destiny and create the future we want.

Destiny could still exist even if we have free will

It is believed by many that our decisions and choices play a role in what our destiny will be, but at the same time, there are many factors outside of our control that can affect our destiny as well.
Ultimately, free will gives us the power to choose our paths, but life, and the outcome of those choices may still be unpredictable.
